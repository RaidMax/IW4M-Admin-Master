Help & Setup

After subscribing to your first plugin, you must enter your subscriber ID into your IW4MAdminSettings.json configuration file.
This is performed by setting the SubscriberId property to the email address you used to subscribe with.

Don't forget to restart IW4MAdmin [after saving your configuration] to load the new plugins.
