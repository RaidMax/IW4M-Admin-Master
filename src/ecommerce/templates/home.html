Store Information

This website serves to provide users of IW4MAdmin [an administration tool for the Call of Duty® series] with an easy-to-use way to subscribe to user created plugin content.
Since it is in a very early development stage, this website will change and improve over time.

How does it work?

Each IW4MAdmin instance is capable of retrieving the collection of plugins that you have subscribed to. These plugins will automatically be kept up-to-date so you don't have to worry about updating your plugins.

How do I get plugins?

Simply navigate to the plugins page to view available plugins.
Next, subscribe to the plugins you are interested in by clicking the subscribe button and filling out your information.
Finally, update your IW4MAdmin configuration as outlined on the help page.
